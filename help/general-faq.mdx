## Getting Started

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="To start a new project/ upload a video on Quso.ai:" icon="sparkles">
    1. **Log in** to your [Quso.ai](http://Quso.ai) dashboard.
    2. From the home page, **select the feature** you’d like to use:
       - **AI Clips** – Automatically generate short highlight videos.
       - **AI Captions** – Add or edit captions for your videos.
       - **Edit a Video** – Launch the full video editor for custom edits.
    3. You’ll see a short description under each feature to help you choose the right one.
    4. **Follow the on-screen prompts** to upload your video or generate content using AI.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where is my dashboard and the video editing platform?" icon="sparkles">
    After login, you're taken to the dashboard by default. If not, click **“Home”** in the side menu. Open any project to access the video editor.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What are the supported video formats and size limits for upload?" icon="sparkles">
    Supported formats: `.mp4`, `.mov`, `.webm` , `.m4v`

    - **Free plan:** Max 5 GB/ 720p
    - **Paid plans:** Up to 15GB / 1080p+
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## AI Video Creation & Editing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I generate AI clips or highlights from my long video?" icon="sparkles">
    1. From the home page, **select the feature**:
       - **AI Clips** – Automatically generate short highlight videos.

       ![FAQ Ai Clips Jp](/images/FAQ-ai-clips.jpg)
    2. You’ll see a short description under each feature to help you choose the right one.
    3. **Follow the on-screen prompts** to upload your video or generate content using AI.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I add or edit subtitles and captions on my video?" icon="sparkles">
    In the editor:

    - Open the **Subtitles** tab
    - Edit any subtitle box directly on the timeline
    - Use the auto-generated captions or edit them manually

    ![FAQ Subtitles Jp](/images/FAQ-subtitles.jpg)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I  background audio?" icon="sparkles">
    In the editor:

    - Navigate to the **Audio** tab
    - upload an audio file (MP3, WAV)
    - Adjust timing in the B-roll tab

    ![Add Music To Clips Audio Jp](/images/add-music-to-clips-audio.jpg)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I rotate or flip a video clip?" icon="sparkles">
    We do not have this feature on our platform.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How can I change the speed of a video or clip?" icon="sparkles">
    - Select the clip you want
    - Click on the download button
    - Over at the download option, you can select the playback speed for the downloaded video

    ![FAQ Download Playback Speed Jp](/images/FAQ-download-playback-speed.jpg)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I save a custom template or preset?" icon="sparkles">
    - After editing, click **Save as Template**
    - You will get the option to name the template before you save it
    - It’ll be available under the **Templates** tab for reuse

    ![FAQ(3) Jp](/images/FAQ\(3\).jpg)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I add new scene cuts in CutMagic?" icon="sparkles">
    In the CutMagic menu in the Editor:

    - Go to the point in the video where you would like to add the scene
    - Click **Add Scene Cut at Current Time**
    - Readjust the frame to your liking; you can also toggle the split-screen option.

    ![FAQ Cutmagic Jp](/images/FAQ-cutmagic.jpg)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why are no clips being generated from the uploaded video?" icon="sparkles">
    Here are some things to keep in mind while uploading videos using AI clips:

    - The video must be over 3 minutes in length
    - The video must have spoken words.
    - Make sure to select the correct language while uploading the video. 

      <Tip>
        If you select English as the language and the video is in French, no clips will get generated.
      </Tip>

    Our platform uses the transcript generated from the videos to create clips.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Plans, Credits & Upgrades

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do credits work in Quso.ai?" icon="sparkles">
    Credits are used whenever you process or create content in [Quso.ai](http://Quso.ai) — such as AI Clips, Captions, or Text-to-Video.

    - **1 credit = 1 minute of video**, rounded up to the nearest minute.
    - Credits are shown **before** any action is processed.
    - On **monthly plans**, credits renew each month and unused credits **expire**.
    - On **annual plans**, you get all credits upfront for the year.
    - **Add-on credits** (purchased separately) **do not expire**.
    - Failed or unprocessed videos are **auto-refunded**.
    - In team workspaces, credits are **shared** across all members.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What does the “Upgrade” message mean?" icon="sparkles">
    You’ve either:

    - Used your free credits
    - Accessed a premium-only feature

    Upgrade to a paid plan to continue.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is the free plan available in my country?" icon="sparkles">
    Yes, but availability may vary based on location or IP. If you can’t access it, contact support.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Will the Plus plan allow me to upload directly to YouTube?" icon="sparkles">
    No, the Plus plan does not have social media features.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why can’t I upload my video?" icon="sparkles">
    Check:

    - Format: `.mp4`, `.mov`, `.webm`
    - File size: under your plan’s limit
    - Browser: try Chrome
    - Clear cache or disable extensions
    - If the issue persists, contact support — it could be a bug
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why did my video processing fail?" icon="sparkles">
    - The file may be too long
    - The server may be overloaded
    - Content may be unsupported (e.g., selecting the wrong language while uploading)
    - If the issue persists, contact support — it could be a bug

    Try again later or split the video.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why can’t I download or play my video?" icon="sparkles">
    - Re-download with a stable internet
    - Make sure your device has storage
    - If the issue persists, contact support — it could be a bug
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why is my video blurry even at 1080p?" icon="sparkles">
    - Check original video quality
    - Re-render if clips were low-res
    - If still blurry, contact support — it could be a bug
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
