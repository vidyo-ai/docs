Quso.ai allows you to **save your editing progress as a draft** so you can pause your work and return to it later without losing any changes.

## ðŸŽ¬ How to Save a Draft

### Step 1: Save Your Work

- At the top of the editor screen, click the **green Save Draft button**.

  ![Draft 1 Jp](/images/draft-1.jpg)

---

### Step 2: Name Your Draft

- Enter a name for your draft so you can easily identify it later.
- For first-time saves, select **Save as New Draft** and click **Save**.

  ![Draft 2 Jp](/images/draft-2.jpg)

---

### Step 3: Save as New or Update Existing

- To update an existing draft, choose the **Update Draft** option.

  ![Draft 3 Jp](/images/draft-3.jpg)

---

### Step 4: Access Your Drafts

- Once saved, your draft will appear in the **Review Clips Drafts** section.
- To access it, click **Back** to return to the list of clips in the Review Clip screen.
- On the left-hand navigation bar, select **Drafts**.

  ![Draft 4 Jp](/images/draft-4.jpg)

---

### Step 5: Continue Editing

- The most recent draft will be displayed.
- Click **Edit** to reopen the editor and continue from where you left off.

  ![Draft 5 Jp](/images/draft-5.jpg)
