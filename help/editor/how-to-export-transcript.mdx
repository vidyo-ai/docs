title: "How to Generate Transcript?"
---

The **Viddy 2.0** feature in Quso.ai allows you to automatically generate transcripts from your uploaded videos. This makes it easy to extract dialogue, create captions, or repurpose your content for blogs and posts.

### ðŸ§­ Step-by-Step Guide

### **Step 1: Open the Viddy Feature**

- Navigate to any of your projects in Quso.ai.
- From the side menu, click on **Viddy**.

![Viddy Preview Project Jp](/images/viddy-preview-project.jpg)

### **Step 2: Select Transcript Option**

- In the Viddy interface, you can either:
  - Enter your own **prompt**, or
  - Choose the **Transcript** option from the available presets.

![Viddy Dashboard Jp](/images/viddy-dashboard.jpg)

Once selected, Quso.ai will process your video and automatically generate the transcript. 

<Info>
  ðŸ’¡ **Note:** There is currently no option to export the transcript directly. To save it, use the **Copy** button available at the end of the generated prompt.
</Info>
