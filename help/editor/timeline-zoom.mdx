### **Step 1: Locate the Zoom Controls**

You can zoom in on the timeline by:

- Clicking the **zoom in/out buttons** near the timeline
- Or using your **mouse scroll wheel** while hovering over the timeline

![Timeline Zoom1 Jp](/images/timeline-zoom1.jpg)

---

### **Step 2: Adjust the End Time of a Clip**

1. Zoom in toward the end of the clip and place the marker at your desired time stamp.
2. Drag the **end marker** to the time stamp point.
3. The clip duration updates accordingly.

![Timeline Zoom2 Jp](/images/timeline-zoom2.jpg)

---

### **Step 3: Add and Position an Image Accurately**

1. Zoom in on the section of the timeline where you want to insert an image.
2. Use the playback bar or timeline ruler to identify the **exact timestamp**.
3. Select the image and place it at that position.

![Timeline Zoom3 Jp](/images/timeline-zoom3.jpg)

---

### **Step 4: Find and Copy an Exact Timestamp**

1. Zoom in as far as needed to see the **frame-accurate timestamp**.
2. Click the **"Copy timestamp"** button (if available) to avoid typing manually.

![Timeline Zoom4 Jp](/images/timeline-zoom4.jpg)

---

### **Step 5: Paste Timestamp to Set Position**

Paste the copied timestamp wherever needed (e.g., when inserting an image or syncing audio). This ensures perfect alignment with other media elements.
