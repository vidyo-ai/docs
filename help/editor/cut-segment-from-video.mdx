title: "How Do I Cut A Segment From The Video?"
---

In this tutorial, you will learn how to remove segments from your video on quso.ai. By the end of this article, you will be able to edit your video clips with precision.

### **Step 1**

Identify the part of the video you want to cut out.

### **Step 2**

Move your cursor to the start of the desired section in the timeline.

![Cut Merge1 Jp](/images/cut-merge1.jpg)

 

### **Step 3**

Click on the Split Selection option.

This action will split your clip at the point where your cursor is.

![Cut Merger2 Jp](/images/cut-merger2.jpg)

### **Step 4**

Decide on the end of the section you want to cut out. 

### **Step 5**

Move your cursor to this point and click on Split Selection again.

![Cut Merge3 Jp](/images/cut-merge3.jpg)

### **Step 6**

Click on the delete option to remove the selected part of the video.

![Cut Merge4 Jp](/images/cut-merge4.jpg)

 

By following these steps, you can cut, split, and merge any part of your video on [quso.ai](http://quso.ai)
