## ðŸŽ¨ Steps to Change Caption Style, Font & Effects

### **Step 1: Open the Editor**

- From your dashboard, click on the **Edit** button for the project you want to customize.

![Edit Captions1 Jp](/images/edit-captions1.jpg)

---

### **Step 2: Access the Subtitles Panel**

- Once inside the editor, locate and click the **Subtitles** tab on the **left-hand side** of the screen.

![Edit Captions2 Jp](/images/edit-captions2.jpg)

---

### **Step 3: Change the Font**

- At the top of the subtitles panel, you can:
  - Choose from **available fonts**
  - Or **upload your own custom font** to match your brand style

![Captions Style4 Jp](/images/captions-style4.jpg)

---

### **Step 4: Customize Subtitle Background Effect**

- Select the **background color** for your captions.
- Click the color block to open the color picker.
- To remove the background color, simply **disable** it by clicking on it and toggling it off.

![Caption Style5 Jp](/images/caption-style5.jpg)

---

### **Step 5: Adjust Radius and Padding for Block Effect**

- Use the slider or input fields to modify:
  - **Border radius** (rounded corners)
  - **Padding** (space around the text inside the caption box)
- To remove the Block effect, simply **disable** it by clicking on it and toggling it off.

![Caption Style6 Jp](/images/caption-style6.jpg)

---

### **Step 6: Apply Outline and Shadow Effects**

- You can layer multiple effects, such as:
  - Shadow
  - Outline
  - Blur
- These effects help your text stand out on various backgrounds.
- To remove these effects, simply **disable** it by clicking on it and toggling it off.

![Caption Style7 Jp](/images/caption-style7.jpg)

---

### **Step 7: Change Font Size**

- Adjust the **text size** using the size slider or input field.

![Caption Style8 Jp](/images/caption-style8.jpg)

---

### **Step 8: Choose a Style Preset**

- Click on the **Styles** tab.
- Browse available **style presets** and click on one to instantly apply it to your subtitles.

![Caption Style9 Jp](/images/caption-style9.jpg)
