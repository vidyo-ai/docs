### **Step 1: Open Your Project in the Editor**

- From your **All Projects** tab, select the video you want to edit.
- Click **Edit** to open it in the editor.
- Go to the **Text** panel depending on what you want to customize.

![Add Text To Video 1 Jp](/images/add-text-to-video-1.jpg)

---

### **Step 2: Change the Font**

- At the top of the panel, you can:
  - **Choose from available fonts**
  - **Upload your own custom font** to match your brand style

![Edit Text Effect 1 Jp](/images/edit-text-effect-1.jpg)

---

### **Step 3: Customize Subtitle Background Effect**

- To enable the background effect, click on it to toggle it on
- Select the **background color** for your captions or text.

![Text Effect 2 Jp](/images/text-effect-2.jpg)

- Click the color block to open the **color picker**.
- To remove the background color, click it again to toggle it **off**.
- You can also edit the Margin, Padding, and Radius for the background.

![Text Effect 3 Jp](/images/text-effect-3.jpg)

---

### **Step 4: Adjust Radius and Padding for Block Effect**

- To enable the block effect, click on it to toggle it on.
- Use sliders or input fields to adjust:
  - **Border radius** (rounded corners)
  - **Padding** (space around text inside the caption box)
- To remove the block effect, toggle it **off**.

![Text Effect 4 Jp](/images/text-effect-4.jpg)

---

### **Step 5: Apply Outline and Shadow Effects**

- Layer multiple effects to make text stand out:
  - **Shadow**
  - **Outline**
  - **Blur**
- To remove an effect, toggle it **off**.

![Text Effect 5 Jp](/images/text-effect-5.jpg)

---

### **Step 6: Change Font Size**

- Adjust the text size by entering a value.

![Text Effect 6 Jp](/images/text-effect-6.jpg)
