Emojis are a great way to add personality, humor, or emotional tone to your video captions. With **Quso.ai**, you can easily **insert emojis manually** into any caption line to enhance viewer engagement and visual appeal.

---

## ✍️ How to Manually Add Emojis to Captions

### **Step 1: Open the Editor**

- From your [Quso.ai](http://Quso.ai) dashboard, open the project you'd like to edit.
- Click **“Edit”** to access the video editor.

### **Step 2: Navigate to the Subtitles Panel**

- Click on the **Subtitles** tab on the left-hand side.
- You'll see a list of all captions with their corresponding timestamps.

![Edit Captions2 Jp](/images/edit-captions2.jpg)

### **Step 3: Add Emojis to Your Captions**

- Click into any caption line and simply **type or paste emojis** directly into the text field.\
  For example:
  - `"This is awesome! 🚀"`
  - `"Let's get started! 😄"`

![Captions Emoji Jp](/images/captions-emoji.jpg)
