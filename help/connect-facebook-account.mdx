Only **Facebook Pages** are supported. Facebook does not support linking your personal Facebook accounts for automatic publishing.

<Tip>
  Please be sure you are the admin of Facebook Page and **grant all permissions** to Pages and selections. Failure to do so might prevent successful posting.
</Tip>

## **Go to the Manage accounts page**

Open the manage accounts page from the social tab and click on the link button. This will open a new tab.

![Social Page Jp](/images/FB-article/social-page.jpg)

## **Click to Link Facebook**

Click the Facebook icon on the Social Accounts page.

![FB Click To Link Jp](/images/FB-click-to-link.jpg)

## **First Time Facebook Linking**

A Facebook sign-in will pop-up - _please be sure you allow pop-ups_.\
If you have already linked Facebook with Ayrshare, skip ahead.

![Facebook Article Login Jp](/images/FacebookArticle-login.jpg)

## **Choose a Facebook Page**

Follow the instructions and select a Facebook Page to link. Click **Continue as NAME** on the login page.

![FB Approval Page Jp](/images/FB-approval-page.jpg)

## **Choose a Facebook Page**

Once back at the Ayrshare dashboard, choose a Facebook page to link and click **Submit**.

![FB Page Select Jp](/images/FB-page-select.jpg)

Your Social Accounts page will now be updated with your Facebook account.

![FB Linked Jp](/images/FB-linked.jpg)
