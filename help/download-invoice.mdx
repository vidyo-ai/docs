## Where can I find my invoices?

You can find your past invoices in the Billing & Plans dashboard in the settings menu. Alternatively, you can also reach out to our support team to help you with this.

### Step 1: Goto the billings and plans menu.

![Upgradedowngradearticle(2) Jp](/Upgradedowngradearticle\(2\).jpg)

### Step 2: Once in the menu, scroll down and you will find all invoices.
