# What is AI Influencer?

AI Influencer lets you create high-quality talking-head videos using lifelike AI avatars—no cameras, studio setup, or actors required. Here’s how it works and how to get started.

![Ai Influencer Jp](/images/ai-influencer.jpg)

## What It Offers

- **80\+ Life-like AI Avatars**\
  Choose virtual presenters from diverse backgrounds to match your audience and style.
- **Script Flexibility**\
  Either upload your own script or prompt AI to write one for you. Then edit it to fit your brand’s tone.
- **Fully Customizable Visuals**\
  Adjust subtitles, visuals, and captions to maintain your brand identity.
- **Multi-Platform Export**\
  Export videos tailored for platforms like Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, LinkedIn, and more.
- **Saves Time & Money**\
  Produce a professional video in minutes—no expensive production sets or scheduling hassles.

## Why Use AI Influencer?

- **Drive Sales & Conversions**: Deliver clear, persuasive messages with a professional avatar.
- **Scale Fast**: Generate more video content without the cost or complexity of video production.
- **Boost Reach**: Experiment with different avatar personas to connect more deeply with specific audiences.

## Get Started Now

Ready to scale your content effortlessly? Navigate to **AI Influencer** in your dashboard, choose your avatar, and start crafting videos that captivate and convert—without any production overhead.
